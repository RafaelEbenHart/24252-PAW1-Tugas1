About Us

PT Furniture Indah is a leading furniture manufacturer and supplier in Indonesia, committed to delivering high-quality and stylish furniture for homes, offices, and commercial spaces. With years of experience in the industry, we blend craftsmanship, innovation, and sustainable materials to create products that enhance comfort and aesthetics. Our mission is to provide durable, elegant, and functional furniture solutions that cater to various customer needs. Whether it's modern minimalism, classic elegance, or custom-made designs, we ensure that every piece meets the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Product

  • Home Furniture: Sofas, dining tables, chairs, beds, wardrobes, and more.
  • Office Furniture: Work desks, ergonomic chairs, storage cabinets, conference tables.
  • Custom Furniture: Personalized designs based on customer preferences and space requirements.
  • Decorative Items: Shelves, mirrors, lamps, and other home accessories.
